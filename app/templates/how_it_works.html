How it works

WhatToWatch is an app that helps you choose a movie randomly, based on only three criteria.

Step 1.
Choose the genre of the movie you would like to watch

Step 2.
Choose the time period that interests you

Step 3.
Select whether you want the searched movie to have an Oscar award

Step 4.
Press >> Search <<

OR

Press >> Shuffle <<
- application will randomly select the first three steps!
